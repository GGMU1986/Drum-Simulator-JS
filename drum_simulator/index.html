About

Below there are 8 gold squares labelled A, S, D, F, J, K, L, and Semicolon (;). Underneath the label is the sound made when that key is pressed on the keyboard. Go ahead, try it, I'll wait.

The sounds made are COWBELL, HIHAT, KICK, OPEN HIHAT, SNARE, TOM, RIDE CYMBAL, and CRASH CYMBAL. Don't know what any of those words mean? Don't fret, click on each sound name to learn more!

Press on keys simultaneously or one at a time, just make some good beats! Once you feel you can hold down a steady beat, click on one of the drumless tracks to your left, and try to add some percussion to the song.

Don't worry about saving up money for studio time, click on RECORD on the top left to save your musical masterpieces!

When you want to switch it up and test your mental capacity, click on MEMORY GAME on the top left, and try your luck at remebering the sequence!
