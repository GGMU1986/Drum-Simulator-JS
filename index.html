Welcome to the Drum Simulator


There are 8 gold squares labelled A, S, D, F, J, K, L, and Semicolon (;).

Underneath the label is the sound made when that key is pressed on the keyboard.

The sounds made are COWBELL, HIHAT, KICK, OPEN HIHAT, SNARE, TOM, RIDE CYMBAL, and CRASH CYMBAL. Don't know what any of those words mean? Don't fret, click on each sound name above to learn more!


Press on keys simultaneously or one at a time, just make some good beats! Once you feel you can hold down a steady beat, click on one of the drumless tracks and try to add some percussion to the song.
Don't worry about saving up money for studio time, just click on RECORD to save your musical masterpieces!
When you want to switch it up and test your mental capacity, click on MEMORY GAME and try your luck at remebering the sequence!

enjoy!
